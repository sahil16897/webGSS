General School Rules-

1. Students are not allowed to go home during the school time, In case of emergency, parents may contact the principal.

2. Students should be habitually clean and always neatly dressed. The school uniform should be worn on all working days.

3. Care must be taken of all school property. No student should damage any school furniture, write or draw anything on walls.

4. The school is not responsible for the loss of valuable articles brought by the children to the school. It is not advisable to bring valuable articles like expensive watches, fountain pens, items of jewellery to school.

5. No shouting, whistling or running in the corridors of the school building is allowed at anytime.

6. Cases of fighting, quarreling, use of foul language, stealing, cheating and theft etc. are severely punished.

7. Students are not allowed to bring mobiles, ipods, CDs.

8. Students are forbidden to bring crackers, explosives, harmful instruments and other dangerous materials to school.

9. It is the duty of all the students to keep their classroom clean.

10. If there is any change in the correspondence address and phone numbers of the students, parents are requested to inform the changes immediately to school.
